About Me

Chris O Adu is a professional British music producer, arranger, engineer and keyboardist. He is the founding producer of MusicNyu.

Chris has worked with many of the best-loved musicians across London and Nigeria including Adetutu VOJ, Bow Road Band, TeeEye, Rhema Family Mass Choir Ibadan Nigeria, Rhema Mass Choir Yaba Lagos Nigeria, Southgate Christian Fellowship London, Mountain of Fire Ministry London and Streams of Mercy CCLC London.

Chris is the owner and Creative Director of MusicNyu Recording Studios, a world-class home production studio in London featuring the latest state-of-the-art digital and analogue recording technology. Chris is an alumnus of MUSON (Music Society of Nigeria) basic music training school for children and adults interested in learning how to sing or play any musical instrument.
